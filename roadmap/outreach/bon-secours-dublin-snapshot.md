## Size and model

- Acute general hospital delivering elective adult-only inpatient and outpatient care for low-risk medical and surgical patients.
- 90 inpatient beds and 70 day care beds.
- Part of the Bon Secours Health System Group (five hospitals).
- HIQA inspection: 29–30 April 2025 (published 16 October 2025).
- Out-patient satellite clinic in Cavan (consultations + limited non-invasive diagnostic tests).

Clinical areas referenced by HIQA (use sparingly in outreach; can help you sound “inside the workflow”):

- Medical Assessment Unit (MAU): ten-bay unit, including two isolation rooms.
- St Brigid’s Ward: general medical and cardiology.
- Sacred Heart Ward: general surgical, orthopaedics and general medical.
